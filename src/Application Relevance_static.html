Application relevance

 
 
Users are expected to get the feel of the measuring process through this animated experiment and are advised to measure in real life practical situations using anthropometric rod sets and anthropometric boards. Moreover, the users may select their own specific landmarks as required.
When privacy chambers are to be made in a big hall, where the partitioned are not expected to touch the ceiling for air circulation and even ambient light distribution in all the cubicles; the


 
minimum height of a privacy barrier may go to 95th percentile of standing eye height from floor so that 95 percent of the population below this eye height level would not be able to look through over the boundary ensuring privacy to the occupants of individual cubicles. Sufficient additional height allowance to the minimum privacy barrier height obtained on the above consideration may be added; it will ensure privacy of both seated and standing occupants.
