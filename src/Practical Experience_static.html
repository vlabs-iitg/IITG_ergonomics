Key to perform the experiment

  • The animated figure below gives a virtual experience of measuring selective landmark heights from floor.   • Click the left hand up/down arrow button to move the horizontal branch of the anthropometric rod up/down to reach the anthropometric landmarks on the figure. When it attains the specific landmark, the name of the dimention appears on the figure itself and the values in different percentiles appear below the figure.
